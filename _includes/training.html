Race Results

On September 29th, Rutgers Triathlon traveled to Rye, NY, and competed in the NECTC Conference Championships, Westchester Olympic Triathlon. After a salty swim, hilly bike, and a tough run, Rutgers managed to score enough points to place 6th at the race, and 7th for the season in the Conference. As a result, Rutgers has secured a spot at Collegiate Nationals this Spring in Tempe, AZ. Congratulations to all who raced!: Neil Verano, Ryan Kennedy, Erin Darreff, Ryan Swift, Kelsey Noll, Zachary Goldfarb, Guilermo Gonzalez, Chris Marrone, Valentina Gordon, Allysa Go, Steven Vitabile, Chris Dinh, Justine Fairman, Samantha Murray, Gwen Morris, Jerry Saluti, Jennifer Therkorn, and Josh Kim.
