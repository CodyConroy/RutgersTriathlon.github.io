Rutgers Triathlon Running at University Park!



Generally, our long runs are out of Werblin or College Ave Gym while our track workouts are at the ~ 800 m loop at University Park.

If you're new to running, the following is a set of guidelines and required and optional items for a sucessful run.

Required items

  • Running Shoes - A good pair of running shoes will take you far (approx. 300-350 mi) !

  • Socks - While some triathletes don't wear socks during a race, they are great for avoiding blisters and keeping feet happy

  • Running clothes - Dress appropriately for the weather!

  • Sunscreen - Protects your skin from dangerous UV radiation during your run

  • Water bottle(s) - For longer runs that need it, or to keep you hydrated during hard track workouts

  • Snacks - If doing a run longer than 1 hour, bring some snacks!

  • Keys - Don't get locked out from your car or house!

  • Watch - Useful for keeping track of splits, distance, etc. (If it's not on Strava, did you actually run?)


Optional Items

  • Cellphone - In the event you get lost or have an emergency; give a good buddy a call.

  • Cash/Credit Card/Wallet - Great for the inevitable moments when you run out of snacks or hydration.

  • Identification - Driver's license, RoadID, etc are always a good idea to keep on you in the event of a serious accident. Stay safe!

  • Sunglasses - Protects your eyes from wind and dust and other debris and keep you looking fresh.

  • Heart Rate Monitor - Great for tracking your heart rate and can be used to measure your effort levels and improvement over time.
