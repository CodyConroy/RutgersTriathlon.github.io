April 21-22, 2017


The Rutgers Triathlon Team raced hard and fast through the Alabama heat at the USAT Collegiate Club Nationals in Tuscaloosa, Alabama! We sent 13 of our finest triathletes to swim in the Black Warrior River, bike on the Jack Warner Parkway and run through the Tuscaloosa Riverwalk. The team placed 62nd Overall with our Women's Team in 70th place and our Men's team in 56th place! Full results here!

This weekend the Rutgers Triathlon team hosted the first Rutgers Indoor Triathlon at the Werblin Recreation Center! We had 17 athletes come out, some doing their first race and others seasoned triathletes! We had a great time hosting and volunteering, and hope everyone enjoyed the event.
