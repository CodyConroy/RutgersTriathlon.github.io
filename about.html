About Us

End of Semester Fun at the Pool!



Welcome to the Rutgers Triathlon Team, we are a growing club sport that is focused on building a close knit group of friends. Our team is all about having fun and getting members as much access to the triathlon and endurance sport community that we can.


Founded in 2010, Rutgers Triathlon is a club sports team of Rutgers University centered on the New Brunswick campus.


Our goal is to create a close-knit group of friends who strive to compete in Triathlon, while pursuing their degree at RU. Our team proudly competes in the Northeast Collegiate Triathlon Conference, and we have sent members to race at the USAT Collegiate National Championships since 2014.
