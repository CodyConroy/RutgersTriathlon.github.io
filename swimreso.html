Swim Resources

Rutgers Triathlon Swimming at Werblin!



Our swim practices take place at the Cook/Douglass Recreation Center. If you need a ride, posting on the FB page is your best bet.

If you're new to swimming, the following is a list of things to bring to practice along with some optional items.

Required items

  • Swimsuit - Any suit designed for lap swimming, generally with minimal drag

  • Towel - You need to dry off somehow

  • Goggles - Marvelous device that helps you see underwater and stare at the lap line.

  • Water bottle - Helps you stay hydrated during the hard sets, don't drink the pool water.


Optional Items

  • Swim Cap - Helps reduce drag, minimizes contact between your hair and chlorine, keeps hair out of your face and makes you look super cool.

  • Swim Buoy - These are used for pulling drills and are available for our use at the pool.

  • Kickboard - Used for kicking drills / sets and are available for our use at the pool.

  • Fins - We recommend using shorter fins as opposed to diving fins. These are great for drills and can help improve ankle flexibility for kicking.

  • Paddles - Can be used for pulling drills or pull strengthening.

  • Watch - Useful for keeping track of splits, distance, etc.

  • Waterproof Sandals - Protect your feet from the wet floor / keep day shoes dry.

  • Lock - Useful for the lockers at any gym. Front desk will have some, but they do run out. Keep your valuables safe!

  • Wetsuit - Keeps you warm in the cold Atlantic ocean and helps you float to boot. Swim once in a pool before race day!
